Interesting Facts about the Moon

  • The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite.
  • It is about 1/6th the size of Earth.
  • The Moon has no atmosphere, which means there is no weather and no sound.
  • It takes about 27.3 days for the Moon to orbit Earth.
  • The Moon's gravitational pull affects Earth's tides.
  • There are phases of the Moon, including new moon, first quarter, full moon, and last quarter.
  • The Moon is about 238,855 miles (384,400 kilometers) away from Earth.
  • Humans first landed on the Moon on July 20, 1969 during the Apollo 11 mission.
  • The Moon has been a subject of human fascination and mythology for centuries.
